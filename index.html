Let's talk product

This has been developed with the belief that exploration is the key any kind of self development. The venture intends to use this faculty to promote artistic growth.To further this, we organise themed group trips to different locations across the country with artists from various domains. This, we believe, creates a connection between evolving, like minded artists and helps them learn through collective experience. These artists then collaborate to create novel art, and these stories are exhibited to reach out to a larger circle.
